As students of the Auburn University Rural Studio, three fellow undergraduates and I designed and built this three-dwelling home for women in Greensboro, Alabama. Our client was Project Horseshoe Farm, a local non-profit dedicated to community and life improvement in Hale County, one of the poorest counties in the United States. The building contains three independent homes, each with a private kitchen and bathroom, and shared laundry and outdoor spaces. It is the first phase of a two-phase project that will eventually have five units and a community space.
