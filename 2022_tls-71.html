The Last Straw is a journal founded in 1992, focused on natural building and alternative design. After falling out of print it was revived in 2022 by a new editorial collective. In addition to being a member of the collective, which involves editing, writing, and illustrating for the journal, I provide web-design at www.thelaststraw.org
