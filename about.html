Hi, I'm Gavin. I work in architecture and design in San Francisco. I'm interested in work that makes buildings and cities more livable, sustainable, and equitable through housing accessibility, reused or bio-based materials, and better labor practices.


I'm an architectural designer at David Baker Architects, an editor of The Last Straw, a board member of the California Straw Building Association, and a very occasional freelance designer. I've also worked as a fabricator in the DBA_Workshop, interned at Wang Architects, and designed and built three homes at the Rural Studio with my classmates.

Everything on here was made by me unless credited otherwise. I designed and coded the website using html and css in Adobe Dreamweaver. It's hosted by hostgator. This site is mostly for fun and only includes fully completed projects :) If you want to see my portfolio and the work I'm doing currently just reach out by Email, LinkedIn, or Instagram
