The Last Straw is a magazine founded in 1992 about natural building that my friends and I revived in 2022. Issue 75 focuses on straw panels. For many natural builders, straw panels represent the old dream of acceptance into conventional construction. In some ways it's starting to happen. As discussed in the issue, there is a steadily increasing demand and a growing body of built projects that demonstrate their efficacy.
