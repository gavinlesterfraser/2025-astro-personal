The Last Straw is a magazine founded in 1992 about natural building that my friends and I revived in 2022. Issue 74 took a deep dive on the the ways that Community Rebuilds operates and affects it's local community. It also featured progressive east coast straw projects and contributions from thoughtful people I respect and admire Massey Burke and Chris Magwood.
